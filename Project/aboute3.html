ABOUT HIGHGRAD E3

Highgrad E3 is a Unique PG development programme
which aims at
  • Educating PGs with High Value Content
  • Enlighten them to perform better in Exams
  • Explore areas not covered in the exam syllabus
Apart from Final Year Curriculum Highgrad E3 will
include sessions on either of the below topics
  • Ethics
  • Effective communication and counseling skills
  • Medico Legal Aspects
  • Setting up of a clinic
  • Next Level Courses
  • Urban vs Rural Practice
